About Us

The idea behind this website is to create a common platform for all the students (Alumni & Current Students) of the university. The current students can get in touch with their seniors (Alumni) to discuss various things and their concerns and at the same time e.g., if any alumni want to share about his startup to all the students, he can directly be in touch with all the students of his/her university. The platform keeps and shows the records of all the students along with preliminary information such as their links to various social media platforms, their basic information about their academia, and some other relevant information. Various other features such as Jobs/Internship opportunities, Events, and various other things will also be shown. Our mission is to increase comunication network in between Freshers and other students to help them develop better career.
